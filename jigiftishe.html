Jigiftishe

Jigiftishe is the a new Tigo promotion that gives customers a chance to win Tsh 1 Million daily, Tsh 10 Million Weekly and a Grand prize of Tsh 50Million for 1st Winner, 25Mil for 2nd Winner and 15Mil for 3rd Winner at the end of the promotion

Jigiftishe gives customers chances to win CASH prizes for every transaction done; such as reload, buy packs or devices, Tigo pesa transactions.

Every transaction done gives 1 chance in the draw

This promotion is open to all Tigo customers nation-wide. BOTH Prepaid customers & Post paid / hybrid

Draw Eligibility

Daily Draw

  • Perform any transaction during that day between midnight and 23:59hrs
  • Transactions can be
  • Reload – all channels - scratch, EPIN, Tigo pesa
  • Buy any pack
  • Transact with Tigo pesa – cash in / cash out, Recharge and buy packages, pay bills, bank transfer, merchant payments Nivushe, loans, Bima subscription, balance check E statements. or P2P
  • Each transaction gives you one chance.
  • The more you transact the more your chances
  • No transaction will be carried over to next day draw

Weekly Draws

  • Perform any transaction during respective week between (Monday 0000hrs – Sunday 23:59hrs)
  • The more you transact the more your chances
  • No transaction will be carried over to next week draw

Grand Draw

  • All your transactions performed during promotion period will enter final draw
  • The more you transact the more your chances
